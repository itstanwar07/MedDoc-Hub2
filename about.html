About the Platform

Advancing Medical Research Together

MedDoc Hub is a dedicated online platform designed to streamline the publication and sharing of medical research across the global academic community.

🏥

Our Mission

We aim to foster collaboration, ensure quality, and accelerate medical advancements by combining automated AI checks with expert human oversight — making it easier than ever to share knowledge that matters. Every approved paper contributes to an open, accessible repository that benefits clinicians, educators, and researchers worldwide.

How Submission Works

Researchers and students can upload their medical papers through a simple, guided form. Each submission requires a title, structured abstract, category tags, and keywords — ensuring every paper is properly indexed for discovery. Uploads are accepted as PDFs and immediately queued for review upon submission.

AI-Assisted Review

Once submitted, an advanced AI model reviews the document for topical relevance, structural formatting, completeness, and basic compliance with our publication standards. The AI produces a structured report that flags any issues and assigns a preliminary category — giving administrators a clear, objective starting point for their evaluation.

Human Oversight & Approval

After the AI examination, a qualified human administrator evaluates the paper alongside the AI report. The administrator makes the final decision: approve for publication, request revisions, or reject with feedback. This dual-layer process ensures no content bypasses expert scrutiny — maintaining the integrity of the repository.
