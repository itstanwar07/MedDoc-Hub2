The Process

From Submission to Publication

A clear, structured pipeline — from your upload to a live published paper.

01
🎓

Student Uploads Paper

A student logs in to their portal and submits a research paper as a PDF, along with the title, abstract, and keywords.

Student Portal
02
🔬

Researcher Submits Too

Researchers and professors can also upload their own papers directly, bypassing the student review queue and going straight to admin.

Researcher Portal
03
🤖

AI Scans & Categorises

Our trained AI bot reads the paper, detects its type — clinical, experimental, review, case study — and automatically assigns it to the correct research category.

AI Engine
04
🛡️

Admin Reviews & Decides

An administrator reviews the paper alongside the AI report, verifies the category and quality, then either approves, rejects, or requests revisions.

Admin Panel

Approved papers go live instantly in the open-access repository, fully searchable and available to the global academic community.
